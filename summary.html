SUMMARY

Description

My visual is an interactive map of soil cover by vegetation, for each field on the Limagne plain, for the period 2018 to 2020. Such output may be useful:

  • to identify plots with high soil cover, which can then lead to field surveys with farmers cultivating these plots to identify best practices to be disseminated;

  • to grant subsidies to farmers on the basis of the soil cover indicator. The cost of establishing intermediate cover is an obstacle to the development of these crops on arable farms without livestock. Hence assessing soil cover is a necessary step in order to define effective policies to promote the adoption of cover crops.

Datasets

For the report and the visual, the following datasets have been used:

  • NASA Shuttle Radar Topography Mission

  • Landsat 8 multispectral satellite images, completed with Sentinel-2 dataset to obtain as much data as possible on the evolution of vegetation in the study area

  • NASA-USDA enhanced SMAP global soil moisture dataset, to identify soil moisture-related obstacles to the adoption of cover crops

In addition, field boundaries for the study area have been extracted from the Land Parcel Identification System file of the Common Agricultural Policy.

Sustainable Development Goals

This proposal is related to two development goals:

  • Zero hunger. The main benefits of soil cover by vegetation for agricultural productivity are largely recognized. Cover cropping increases soil fertility. It increases soil microbial abundance, activity, and diversity. Winter soil cover slows down surface runoff, thus limiting erosion while nutrient uptake by plants reduces losses due to leaching when soils are saturated with water.

  • Climate action. Increasing soil cover by vegetation provides an opportunity to increase soil carbon storage thanks to an overall increase in the photosynthetic activity of agricultural systems.

List of tools

The following tools have been used for this submission:

  • Google Earth Engine for data extraction

  • R for data analysis

  • R, with Quarto, for interactive data visualization and creation of this website

Data and codes are available on this GitHub repo.
